Conclusion

conclusion

In conclusion, ...

Lecture 01: There are theoretically coherent models of instrumental action,

one involving goal-directed processes and the other habitual processes.

Lecture 02: ... and each does actually explain some actions!

This seems to be a problem for a standard philosophical view about what actions are.

challenge

Discover why people act,
individually and jointly.

problem

The standard philosophical framework rules out the dual-process theory of instrumental action a priori.

The dual-process theory of instrumental action provides no view about what actions are.
